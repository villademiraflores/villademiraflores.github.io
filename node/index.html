JetSpeed 5000

Excellent and powerful engine for medium-sized jets. Designed and created by a team of experienced mechanics, engineers and aviation experts.

  • Compact design
  • Excellent thrust-to-weight ratio, low power consumption

  • Weight: 90.5 kg
